⚡ The February Experiment: Causality, Not Correlation
When ad spend dropped 90% in February (₹8.27L → ₹0.86L), GMV dropped 87.5% in the same month. This is not coincidence — it is proof that advertising directly generates the majority of platform revenue. The demand exists; advertising converts it to sales.
🛡️ Advertising is Retaining Share in a Declining Market
Overall GMV has declined from ₹1.44Cr/month (Q4) to ₹0.93Cr (March) despite consistent spend. But without advertising, GMV would be ₹14L/month — a 90% collapse. Advertising is actively preventing category share from eroding in the face of market headwinds.
🎯 SPA at 17–30× ROAS: The Workhorse Campaign
A single SPA "All Products" campaign has consistently delivered 17–30× ROAS across every active month — Q4 at 29.88×, January at 24.19×, March at 17.81×. Even as overall GMV declines, this campaign maintains exceptional efficiency and should be protected from budget cuts.
