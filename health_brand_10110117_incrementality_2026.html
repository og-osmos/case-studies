🎯 SPA = Pure Incremental Demand Capture
At 38–42× ROAS in Jan–Feb, the SPA campaign captured ₹18–20L GMV from just ₹47K/month. On a pharmacy platform, search clicks are near-100% incremental — shoppers are in-market and will buy from whoever appears. No ad = competitor gets the sale.
📊 Flat GMV = Full Market Capture, Not Failure
The category's addressable demand on this platform is approximately ₹23L/month. Advertising at ₹47K SPA captures essentially all of it. GMV not growing is a category signal — the brand's job is to hold position, not lose share to competitors who are bidding on the same keywords.
⚠️ March Scale-Up Damaged April — Action Needed
Inflating SPA to ₹5.17L in March did not grow GMV but collapsed ROAS from 42× to 3×. April is now projecting ₹7.73L — a 67% drop. The SPA campaign needs to reset to its efficient ₹47K/month budget. Every week of degraded efficiency deepens the GMV hole.
