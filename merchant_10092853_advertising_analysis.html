The strategic story behind the numbers

Merchant 10092853 deployed a disciplined, compounding ad investment strategy from a modest ₹5.0L launch in April 2025 to a peak of ₹170L in March 2026 — a 3103× growth in monthly spend. Despite this rapid scale-up, attributed ROAS was consistently maintained in the 10–20x range across all 12 months, a rare hallmark of algorithmically-optimised, incrementally-scaled ad investment.

During high-competition windows (May–Jun 2025 and Sep–Oct 2025), the brand doubled down on spend rather than retreating, which correlated directly with peak attributed GMV quarters (Q3 2025: ₹62.3 Cr, Q4 2025: ₹83.7 Cr). This pattern confirms that advertising acted as a demand-protection mechanism — not merely an acquisition channel.
