🔍 Search ≠ Display in Health & Pharma
Vitamins are bought on intent — shoppers search "Supradyn" or "multivitamin" specifically. Display reaches passive browsers. 13.28L impressions in March produced just 2,601 clicks (0.2% CTR) — confirming display cannot substitute for search in this category.
⚡ The Fix is Operational, Not Strategic
KW Generic SPA delivered 1.67× ROAS for 6+ consecutive months. It is paused, not deleted. Reactivating at ₹1.5–2L/month restores ₹3L+ GMV and 5L+ search impressions within the same billing cycle.
🏁 Every Week Dark = Steeper Re-entry Cost
Competitors have now accumulated 2+ months of performance history on the same keywords with stronger Quality Scores and better placements. The longer the gap, the higher the re-entry bid required to reclaim position.
