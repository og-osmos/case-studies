📈 SPA Was Already Working — Display Made It Work Harder
Huggies ran strong SPA through all 14 months — averaging ₹5.5L/month in SPA spend and a healthy 17–23× ROAS in SPA-only periods. Display was not deployed to rescue a failing channel. It was deployed to amplify one that was already winning.
🎯 The Two Best Months Both Had Display Running
January 2026 (₹160.8L GMV) and February 2026 (₹152.9L GMV) are the two highest GMV months in the dataset — both had SPA and Display running simultaneously. The previous peak without display was ₹131.7L in November 2025, a 22% lower ceiling.
🔄 Every Display Relaunch Lifted the Ceiling
Display launched → GMV spiked (Apr–May 2025: ₹115L). Display paused → GMV plateaued (Jul–Nov 2025: ₹87–132L). Display relaunched → GMV hit new highs (Dec–Feb: ₹155–161L). The pattern repeated twice with the same result both times.
💡 Mar '25 ROAS Caveat
March 2025 shows an artificially low combined ROAS (6.6×) because SDA launched with ₹189K spend in just 8 days while SPA was paused. This is a transition artefact — display attribution matured significantly in the weeks that followed.
Incrementality Analysis

Display Lifted SPA Efficiency by 19%. Same Spend. Better Results.

Controlling for SPA spend level: in October and November 2025 (SPA-only, ₹7.3L avg/month), SPA ROAS averaged 17.75×. In January and February 2026 (SPA+Display, ₹7.1L avg SPA spend/month), SPA ROAS averaged 21.1×. Display created a 19% halo uplift on the SPA channel — before counting its own attributed GMV.

🔬 How the Halo Works
Display ads serve shoppers earlier in the purchase journey — category pages, brand pages, and re-engagement placements. When a shopper sees a Huggies display ad first, then later searches and clicks a SPA listing, the SPA gets the last-click credit. Display warms up demand that SPA converts — making every SPA rupee stretch further.
📊 21× Effective ROAS on Display Spend
In Jan+Feb 2026, display spent ₹2.96L and directly attributed ₹15.9L GMV (5.4× direct ROAS). But the SPA halo uplift added an estimated +₹47L in extra SPA GMV at the same SPA spend levels — bringing effective display ROAS to ~21×. Display earns at parity with SPA when the full halo is counted.
📦 Product Lines Defended by Display
SDA campaigns covered NatureSoft (NS) Listing Page and NS PDP — Huggies' premium nappy line. Category page display ensures Huggies appears at the very top of the funnel when parents browse "diapers", before competitors can intercept with SPA at the search stage.
🚀 The Recommended Path: Keep Both On
Periods with SDA off saw SPA ROAS range 16–19×. Periods with SDA active saw SPA ROAS range 20–24×. The gap widens at higher SPA spend. Scaling SPA to ₹8–9L/month while keeping SDA at ₹1.5–2L would maximise the halo multiplier and push GMV toward ₹180–200L/month.
